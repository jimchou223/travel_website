Overview

Locating at the border of tropical and sub tropical zone, makes Taiwan a typical the humid subtropical climate. The humidity will strengthen the temperature, sometimes you will feel hotter or colder than the real temperature. The typical typhoon season is June to September, if you are planning to visit Taiwan during that season, you'd better keep your eyes on the weather forecast. Sometimes typhoon causes damage to a certain degree and the transportation will be a mess. But without that, Taiwan is absolutely a great place to visit.

Spring

Spring is a great seaon to visit Taiwan if you enjoy easy breeze and flower bloom. Especially the Cherry Blossom during March to April. There are several places like Alishan Cherry Blossom Festival, Sun Moon lake, Formosan Aboriginal Culture Village you can find more than 2000 Blossoming trees. Another famous events about flowers is Calla Lily Festival at Yangmingshan. Occupying more than 13 hectares, are open to visitors for blooming season in March or April.

  • When: March to May.
  • Temperature: Average lows are between 15ºC and 22ºC (58ºF and 71ºF) and average highs are between 19ºC and 27ºC (65ºF and 79ºF)
  • What to wear: T-shirt will be perfect for you if you are from north country like Canada but Don't forget to put a jacket in your bag. It will definately do you good if you put an umbrella or raincoat in your bag.

Summer

Alough it might very hot during summer time in Taiwan, just like any other tropical island, you can still experience the enthusiasm and enjoy the beautiful sunshine, blue sky, ocean in the south Taiwan like Kenting. Ready to get tanned?

  • When: June to August
  • Temperature: Average lows are between 24ºC and 28ºC (72ºF and 82ºF) and average highs are between 27ºC and 31ºC (80ºF and 87ºF). The crazy humidity pushes those highs even higher.
  • What to wear: During the stemming and hot summer in Taiwan, T-shirt, shorts, flip flop, sandals will be your best choices. Still don't forget the umbrella or raincoat. There might be a sudden thuderstorm in the afternoon even it's a big sunny day.

Fall

The weather is cooler but still warm and sunny, and October and November has relatively less rainfall, and it is still warm.

  • When: September to November
  • Temperature: Average lows are between 18ºC and 27ºC (64ºF and 80ºF) and average highs are between 27ºC and 30ºC (80ºF and 85ºF).
  • What to wear:Long sleeves shirt seems to be a good option. Somehow durnig the early fall, there still are some hot days over 30ºC (85ºF) so T-shirt with light jacket might be a better option.

Winter

Winter is the best time to explore delicious food in Taiwan. There are a lot of tradition local food and it will be a better idea to enjoy them during winter like lamb hot pot,

  • When: December to February
  • Temperature: Average lows are between 13ºC and 15ºC (55ºC and 58ºC) and average highs are between 17ºC and 19ºC (61ºF and 65ºC).
  • What to wear: Long sleeves, jeans, functional Jackets or parka are what you need. Due to the humidity, you may feel colder than the real temperature. Plus there are no heater in most places and transportation so you'd better wear more.
