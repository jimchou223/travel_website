Introduction


Enjoy the chill music during planning your trip to Taiwan!

Taiwan is an island of unexpected beauty. It also has a mysterious name called "Formosa". In order to realize the meaning behind this mysterious name, we need to go back to 16th century, the Age of Discovery. At that time a Portuguese ship accidentally sailed through the Taiwan Strait. The sailors were astonished by the beautiful scenery they saw, one of them couldn't help but shouted "Ilha Formosa", means a beautiful island in English. That is the history behind the name. Just like the meaning of that name, Taiwan is an island of beauty. In the country side, you can find small villages surrounded by hills and forests to roam in, it's an ideal destination for those who love to spend their vacation exploring the outdoors. Not only the natural scenery, Taiwan is also famous for the diversity, especially when it comes to food, religion, architecture, language, living habits. Taiwan was ruled by different countries like Netherlands, Spain, Japanese. Due to the complex historical events and background, it's a place filled with cultures. Taiwanese people mixed those different culture and shape them into the way it look and feel like in Taiwan now.

Location

Taiwan is located in between the southeastern coast of the Asian continent and the East Asian islands. Japan and the Ryukyu Islands is at the North east side; to the south are the Philippine Islands; to the west is Mainland China. Taiwan is a long and narrow island stretching from north to south. The island is about 395 km long vertically and about 144 km in width with a total land area of around 36,000 square kilometers (about 14,400 square miles). Center Taiwan is occupied with steep mountains; therefore, great altitude differences occur throughout the island. The mountains mostly run from north to south. The highest point is Yushan Peak of the Yushan Mountain Range, which reaches an elevation of 3,952 meters above sea level making it the highest peak in Northeast Asia.
